Solar-Powered Water Pumps

Home › Solutions › Solar-Powered Water Pumps
Keeping your solar panels clean does not have to be a difficult proposition. In fact it couldn't be easier when you install a cleaning system from Geoken.

Solar Water Pumping Systems is a stand-alone system operating on power generated by Solar Photovoltaic Panels. Solar water pumps are specially designed to lift water for irrigation, farms, garden and much other similar application the system is easy to install and helps a lot in irrigation process due to its functionality, cost effectiveness and eco friendliness also it is easy to maintain. The economy and reliability makes it an excellent choice for the main water pumping system and it also minimizes the future cost and uncertainties. The whole of the system is easily maintainable or one can say completely maintenance-free. The life of this product is competitively high and hence is highly reliable.

It also supports dry running protection which is an optional case. Controlling of the pump systems and monitoring the operating states is easy an user friendly. It is also protected against polarity, overload and high temperature.

Solar Operation: Integrated MPPT (Maximum Power Point Tracking) Max. efficiency 92 % (motor + controller)

Pump:

Mostly AC submersible pumps with different HP ratings are used for irrigation purpose. A submersible pump is immersed in water. These pumps are designed for high head and medium flow application.

Pump Controller:

An electronic device which matches the PV power to motor parameter & regulates the operations. It can be used on any AC 3 Ø pump, like Submersible Pump of given HP rating. Pump Controller is designed with MPPT technology and VFD to maximize water delivery at various solar energy levels. It has arrangement to accept 3 Ø ac input from grid so that you can run pump at full speed whenever solar energy is not sufficient.

  • The MPPT converts PV power into high-current and low-voltage power which satisfies the pump motor characteristics and therefore maximizes the system efficiency.
  • VFD allows operating motors & pumps at the precise speed needed for your process. With the ability to vary motor and pump speed, system efficiency is increased.
PV Array:

An array of PV modules connected in series & possibly strings of modules connected in parallel. A number of PV modules are combined as an array to meet different energy demands.
