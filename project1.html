Project Overview

For isolated communities living far away from electricity grid, life can be difficult. The cost of using kerosene lamps is rising and simply charging a mobile phone can mean a trek to the nearest charge point.

De Ferranti supplies small solar power plants which, thanks to their mini grid, can distribute power across an entire community.

The main advantage of this type of system is its flexible modular design, making it scalable and expandable as needs grow and more funds become available. It can also be supplemented by wind, hydro or generator power to ensure a constant supply of electricity.

De Ferranti Ltd is a leading engineering company specializing in renewable energy, electrical engineering, and construction services.
